Improving the visibility through Search Engine Optimization (SEO)

Improving a website's visibility on search engine results pages (SERPs). Focuses on on-page (content, keywords) and off-page (backlinks) optimization. Tools: Google Analytics, SEMrush, Ahrefs.

Content Marketing, strategy that creating and sharing relevant digital content

Creating valuable, relevant content to attract and engage a target audience. Includes blog posts, infographics, eBooks, videos, and whitepapers.Focused on storytelling and problem-solving.

Social Media Marketing on which users build social networks and share information

Promoting brands and products on platforms like Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, and Twitter. Strategies include organic posts, paid ads, influencer partnerships, and community engagement.

Email Marketing to generate leads, nurture clients, and build relationships

Sending personalized emails to nurture leads, retain customers, and promote products. Includes newsletters, automated campaigns, and event invitations.

Pay-Per-Click (PPC) Advertising

Running paid ads on platforms like Google Ads, Bing Ads, and social media. Charges advertisers only when users click on the ad. Formats: search ads, display ads, shopping ads, and video ads.
